Golden Eye

Golden Eye is a web service that tracks and collects current exchange rates from public APIs. Here you can view the exchange rates of such currencies as the Ukrainian hryvnia (UAH), US dollar (USD), euro (EUR) and bitcoin (BTC). All indicated exchange rates are periodically automatically updated and it is also possible to update them manually, together and separately. This website also provides its own API for receiving exchange rates in formats such as JSON and XML.

External APIs:

  • PrivatBank API: https://api.privatbank.ua/#p24/exchange
  • Monobank API: https://api.monobank.ua/docs/
  • CoinAPI: https://www.coinapi.io/
